About Me

Profile

I am a Professor of Information Technology and Operations Management at the Cox School of Business, Southern Methodist University.
My research spans product development, technology and innovation management, supply chains, platforms, and operational strategy.

Education

  • Ph.D. — McCombs School of Business, UT Austin (2006) : NPD, Innovation, Supply Chain Management
  • MBA — Indian Institute of Management, Calcutta (2000) :Operations Management, Marketing, Information Systems
  • B.Tech. — Indian Institute of Technology Madras (1998) :Mechanical Engineering
