About

I am a Professor of Information Technology and Operations Management at the Cox School of Business, Southern Methodist University. My work spans product development, technology and innovation management, platforms, and operational strategy.

Research interests

  • Product development and innovation
  • Technology & entrepreneurship (deep tech, experimentation)
  • Operations strategy and market design
  • Platforms and information economics
